Crystal Collector!

Click the crystals to collect points and win!

Rules of the Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Each crystal holds an unknown value. When you click a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown on the screen. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
